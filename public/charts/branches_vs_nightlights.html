Bank branches vs nighttime light intensity

Each dot is an Indian district. Horizontal axis: mean nighttime light radiance from VIIRS satellite imagery in 2023 (a proxy for economic activity / electrification). Vertical axis: number of bank branches in the district per RBI's DBIE database. Both axes log-scale.

n = districts. Sources: RBI DBIE Banking Outlet Locator · VIIRS Annual Nightlights via SHRUG v2.1 (Development Data Lab, CC BY-NC-SA 4.0). Plaintext citations: Asher, Lunt, Matsuura, Novosad — World Bank Economic Review 2021.
