SLBC Rajasthan — Source Audit
==============================
Audited 2026-05-11 against https://slbcrajasthan.in/

Discovery Pages
---------------
1. https://slbcrajasthan.in/SLBC_Meeting_Held.aspx — Meeting archive (PDF only)
2. https://slbcrajasthan.in/Annexture.aspx — Latest data annexures (PDF historically, XLSX from Sep 2025)

KEY FINDING — Excel archive does NOT go back
--------------------------------------------
slbcrajasthan.in only began publishing the structured XLSX annexure bundles
(Annex 1-12, 13-20, 21-33) with the 167th meeting (Nov 2025 -> Sep 2025 data).
Prior quarters were published as scanned PDF Annexures or as scanned PDF
single-files. The Annexture.aspx page is a "current quarter" page — it overwrites
the same filenames each cycle, so earlier XLSX versions are not preserved
on the server, and Wayback Machine has no snapshots of the XLSX archive.

Therefore: the dedicated SLBC Rajasthan site does NOT have a deeper Excel
archive analogous to Madhya Pradesh's. Backfill beyond Sep 2025 + Dec 2025
is not feasible from this source without OCR of scanned PDFs (out of scope:
pdftotext + openpyxl + urllib only).

Verification of agenda PDF content
----------------------------------
- Checked 166th Agenda (1.7 MB, 37 pages, text-native): zero district-wise
  data rows. No mentions of Ajmer/Alwar/Jaipur in tables — references like
  "Detailed Bank-wise advances is as per Annexure-2 & district wise deposit,
  advances & CD ratio as per Annexure-3" indicate the data is in separate
  annexure files (not in the agenda).
- Checked 165th Minutes: same — no district-wise tables.
- Checked currently-live Annexures-1/2/3.pdf on site (June 2025 data):
  scanned IMAGES (pdfimages shows gray, 8 bpc, 2481x3506 raster). Not
  text-extractable with pdftotext.

Wayback Machine search
----------------------
CDX search for slbcrajasthan.in confirms:
- 4 snapshots of /Annexture.aspx between Mar 2025 and Jan 2026
- 2025-03-16 + 2025-05-24 snapshots: Annexures 1-12, 13-19, 20-33 are all PDFs (scanned)
- 2025-09-10 snapshot: same — Annexures-1/2/3 PDFs (scanned)
- Only the 2026-01-23 snapshot (post-167th meeting) shows the XLSX files for the first time
- No Wayback captures of historical XLSX files in /Raj_Pdf/ at all
- BC.xlsx, SHG.xlsx and similar are bank-wise aggregates (not district-wise FI indicators) — not useful for our schema

Conclusion / Recommendation
---------------------------
- The 2-quarter limit reflects the actual source coverage, not a gap in our
  pipeline. The existing extractor (extract_rajasthan.py) is comprehensive
  and captures 18+ category tables from the 6 XLSX files.
- To deepen Rajasthan coverage in FINER, future options are:
  (a) Wait for SLBC to publish quarter 169 (Mar 2026 data) in XLSX form
  (b) OCR the scanned Annexures PDFs (requires new OCR pipeline — out of
      scope for this task)
  (c) Request CSV/Excel exports from SLBC Rajasthan directly (Indian
      Overseas Bank is the SLBC convener; mail address on site)

No additional files were downloaded as part of this audit — the existing
6 XLSX files in raw/ remain the complete corpus.
