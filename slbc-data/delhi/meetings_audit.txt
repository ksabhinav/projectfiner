Delhi SLBC — meetings audit
============================================================

Source: https://slbcdelhi.pnb.bank.in/
Convenor: Punjab National Bank (since April 2020; previously
          Punjab & Sind Bank was the convenor).
State LGD code: 7
11 revenue districts: Central, East, New Delhi, North, North
  East, North West, Shahdara, South, South East, South West, West.

Granularity: district-wise (all 11 revenue districts), NOT
NCT-aggregate only.  Each quarterly agenda booklet's first
agenda page carries a 'District wise analysis of NCT of Delhi'
table with five numeric columns:
    Branches | ATMs | Deposits | Advances | CD Ratio

Unit conventions:
  - Meetings 110th–121st: amounts in Crores (page-1 summary).
  - Meeting 122nd: Annexure-5 reports same five columns
    but with deposits/advances in actual rupees (×10^7 of crores).
  Extractor auto-detects via median-deposit threshold and
  converts everything to ₹ Lakhs (FINER canonical).

Not extracted (annexures with bank-wise rather than district-wise
granularity, parked for future expansion):
  - PMJDY accounts (Annexure-7+)  — public/private bank rows,
    not district rows
  - KCC (Annexure-3, 14)          — bank-wise only
  - MUDRA (Annexure-12)           — bank-wise only
  - SHG (under 'NULM/DAY-NRLM')   — bank-wise
  - PMEGP / Stand Up India / APY  — bank-wise targets

(Delhi SLBC publishes district-wise rural-branch counts in a
separate table, and district-wise APY targets — they're noted
here but not yet imported.  CD-ratio + branch counts are the
two indicators with comprehensive 11-district coverage.)

Known source-side anomalies (preserved as-is, NOT corrected):
  - 113th meeting (Sep 2023): per-district advances column
    appears truncated relative to the reported overall CDR
    (per-district advances sum to ~₹964,846 Cr, implying
     65.49% CDR; but the booklet footer reports 103.77%).
    Per-district CD-ratio % values are independently printed
    in the same row and used as-is for that quarter.
    Faithful to source per FINER data-integrity rules.
