Granularity finding
-------------------
ONLY consistently district-wise data across all 23 quarters: BANKING NETWORK
(Agenda No. 3 / "Banking Scenario of the State"). Format:
  Type             Banks   North Goa (Rural / SU)   South Goa (Rural / SU)   Total
  Public Sector    12      ...                      ...                      ...
  ...
  Grand Total      48      <north_total>            <south_total>            <state_total>

Pre-2021 agendas (110-113) lack the Rural/SU split and end with "Total" not
"Grand Total" — same 4-column shape (banks, north, south, total) is handled
by a fallback regex in extract_goa.py.

State-aggregate only (BANK-WISE, NOT DISTRICT-WISE):
- Deposits, Advances, CD Ratio
- PMJDY
- KCC
- SHG / Bank linkage
- MUDRA / PMMY
- APY / Social Security
- Education loan
- Housing / PMAY
- All Aadhaar-seeding & authentication data

This is the same structural pattern as Madhya Pradesh and partly Andhra Pradesh
in FINER: a state whose SLBC reports most categories per-bank rather than
per-district. Branch counts are the only quarterly per-district metric.

ACP target (Agenda No. 9): N/S split, but only appears in Q4 (Dec-quarter)
agendas (annual planning). Not ingested — annual cadence, FINER timeseries
schema assumes quarterly.

FLC camp counts: aggregated "491 camps in North Goa and 463 Camps in South Goa"
mentioned in narrative — no per-quarter structured table.

Goa Branch counts also have a known SLBC reporting anomaly in 116th–121st
meetings (Dec 2021 → Mar 2023): SFB Rural jumps from 0 to 101 then back to 0,
inflating the totals (~1024-1031 vs the ~770-810 baseline). The data is
faithful to source — likely an accounting change re: FI Sub-Service Area
touchpoints, undone in the 122nd meeting.
